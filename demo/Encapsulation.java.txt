**Class: Area**

**Purpose:**
Calculates the area of a rectangle.

**Fields:**

* **length:** The length of the rectangle.
* **breadth:** The breadth of the rectangle.

**Constructor:**

* **Area(int length, int breadth):** Initializes the length and breadth fields with the specified values.

**Methods:**

* **getArea():** Calculates and prints the area of the rectangle.

**Class: Main**

**Purpose:**
Demonstrates the use of the Area class.

**Method:**

* **main(String[] args):**
    * Creates an instance of the Area class with a length of 5 and a breadth of 6.
    * Calls the getArea() method on the Area instance to calculate and print the area.
